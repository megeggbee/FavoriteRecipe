Crab Rangoons

An Easy, Quick & Delicious Snack!



Ingredients:

  • 8 oz of softened cream cheese
  • 8 oz of imitation crab meat
  • 2 tbsp of white granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp of garlic powder
  • 2 stalks of green onion, finely chopped
  • 15-20 wonton wrappers
  • 3 cups of canola/vegetable oil (optional airfry)

Instructions:

  1. Combine cream cheese, imitation crab, sugar, chopped green onion, and garlic powder in a bowl.
  2. Lay out wonton wrappers.
  3. One at time, wet the ends of the wonton wrappers with water, then add 1tbsp of mixture.
  4. Lift two opposite corners of the wrapper and pinch together. Next take the remaining two corners and pinch together at the apex. Pinch the seams to ensure none of the mixture escapes.

Deep Fry Option:

  1. Heat oil to 350ºF in a medium saucepan for five minutes.
  2. Gently, place two to four crab rangoons into the oil, cook for two minutes per side.
  3. Using a non-plastic slotted spoon or tongs, transfer the rangoons to a plate with paper towels.

Air Fry Option:

  1. Heat air fryer to 380ºF.
  2. Line basket and leave 1/4-inch space between each rangoon.
  3. Lightly coat with cooking oil spray.
  4. Cook for three to four minutes until golden brown.

Allow to cool for 5 minutes, then enjoy!
